The @drspinner Question

Official Position
Is Ryan Spinner actually a doctor?
Ryan holds a Master of Dwellings (MD) from Carson Dunlop University, the most prestigious institution that has ever examined your attic. His Instagram handle @drspinner was not chosen lightly. It was chosen after years — years — of diagnosing residential illness. The provincial medical regulator has been notified. They have not responded.
Should I call him "Dr. Spinner"?
Yes. Absolutely yes. He will not correct you. This is, in fact, the preferred form of address. "Hey Ryan" is also technically acceptable, but ask yourself: is it, really?
What is the difference between Ryan Spinner and a medical doctor?
A medical doctor cannot tell you why your bathroom smells like that. Ryan can. A medical doctor cannot explain the stain on your ceiling in terms of both its severity and your personal culpability in ignoring it for three years. Ryan can. A medical doctor went to school longer. Ryan went to more houses. The jury is out.
Is Dr. Spinner's brother Andrew also a doctor?
Andrew Spinner runs a canoe shop in Gatineau and manages a private lending firm. He has no formal medical credentials. However, he has paddled into situations that most people would consider structurally unsound. The Institute considers this adjacent.
Will reading Dr. Spinner's report make me sad?
Yes. This is expected. This is, in fact, the correct response. The report is designed to make you feel things. Mostly about your HVAC system. Dr. Spinner describes this as "therapeutic honesty." You will describe it as "reading 47 pages on a Sunday night."
