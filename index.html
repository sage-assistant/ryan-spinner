Background · Who Is Ryan Spinner
For Context
Understand Who They Were Dealing With

Ryan Spinner is a home inspector in Toronto. He is competent. He is professional. He is, by all accounts, a man who has never failed an inspection — because houses don't get to tell him what the problem is. He tells the house.

At Camp Kadimah, where Ryan spent his formative summers as a counsellor, he was known as The Spin Doc. This nickname was not given. It was recognized — the way a title is recognized, by unanimous and unspoken agreement. The man could navigate any situation. He never lost.

He has a brother named Andrew who owns a canoe shop. Andrew has described Ryan's abilities simply, and accurately, as: "Yeah, that's Ryan."

This is the man TD Bank Canada chose to put on hold.

Opening Statement · The Inquiry
TD Bank did not know who they were dealing with. To be fair to TD Bank, TD Bank does not know who anyone is dealing with. TD Bank is dealing with the hold queue. Everyone is in the hold queue. The hold queue is eternal and it does not care about your nickname.
